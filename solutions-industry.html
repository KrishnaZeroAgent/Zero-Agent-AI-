AI identity risk, by industry

Every regulated industry is deploying AI agents into sensitive systems. Zero-Agent brings the governance those environments demand.

Financial Services

The risk

Trading, fraud, and service agents touch regulated data and money movement — a single over-privileged non-human identity can breach SOX, PCI, or customer trust.

How Zero-Agent helps

Least-privilege enforcement and immutable audit trails for every agent, with segregation-of-duties controls auditors and regulators expect.
