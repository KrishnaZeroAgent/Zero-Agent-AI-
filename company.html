Why We Started Zero-Agent

From a problem to a category

Problem

Ungoverned AI

AI agents are rapidly entering enterprise environments without dedicated identity governance.

Insight

Legacy IAM falls short

Traditional IAM, PAM, and IGA platforms were never designed for autonomous machine identities.

Solution

Governance for agents

Identity governance, policy enforcement, least privilege, approvals, and continuous monitoring — built for AI.

Vision

The identity layer for AI

Become the global identity layer for autonomous AI.

Mission

Protect every autonomous AI agent through identity-first security.

Building secure, governed, and trusted AI identities across every enterprise environment.

Vision

Enable organizations to trust autonomous AI through secure identity, governance, and continuous authorization.

Creating the global identity layer that allows autonomous AI to operate safely at enterprise scale.
